## üì± For Mobile Users (Android)

### Quick Setup (5 minutes)

#### Step 1: Install Google Text-to-Speech
1. Open **Google Play Store**
2. Search for **"Google Text-to-Speech"**
3. Tap **Install** or **Update**
4. Wait for installation to complete

#### Step 2: Download Filipino Voice
1. Open **Android Settings**
2. Go to **System** ‚Üí **Languages & Input** ‚Üí **Text-to-Speech**
3. Tap on **Google Text-to-Speech Engine**
4. Tap **Settings** ‚Üí **Install voice data**
5. Find and download **Filipino (Philippines)**
6. Also download **English (United States)** for English stories

#### Step 3: Set as Default
1. Go back to **Text-to-Speech** settings
2. Make sure **Google Text-to-Speech Engine** is selected
3. Tap **Play** to test the voice

#### Step 4: Use in PixelTales
1. Open **PixelTales** app
2. Go to **Library** and open any story
3. Tap the **speaker icon** to start narration
4. Tap the **gear icon** for TTS settings
5. Select your preferred **Filipino voice**
6. Adjust **speed** to 0.9x or 1.0x for natural storytelling
7. Enjoy!

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Problem: "No voices available" in TTS settings

**Solution:**
1. Tap the **"Install TTS Engine"** button in the app
2. This opens Play Store to Google Text-to-Speech
3. Install and download voice data (see steps above)
4. Restart PixelTales app
5. Voices should now appear

---

### Problem: Voice sounds robotic

**Solution:**
1. Make sure you installed **Google Text-to-Speech** (not default Android TTS)
2. Download the **latest voice data** for your language
3. Select a **"Neural"** or **"Wavenet"** voice if available
4. Adjust **speed to 0.9x** for more natural pacing

---

### Problem: No Filipino voices showing

**Solution:**
1. Open Android Settings ‚Üí System ‚Üí Languages & Input
2. Go to Text-to-Speech ‚Üí Google TTS ‚Üí Settings
3. Tap "Install voice data"
4. Make sure **Filipino (Philippines)** is downloaded
5. Restart PixelTales app

---

### Problem: Voice cuts off mid-sentence

**Solution:**
1. Make sure app has internet connection (for first use)
2. Close other apps that might be using audio
3. Turn up device volume
4. Restart the story narration

## üé® Settings Tips

### Optimal Settings for Storytelling:

**Speed:** 0.9x to 1.0x
- Too fast: Hard to follow
- Too slow: Boring
- 0.9x: Great for kids
- 1.0x: Perfect for most stories

**Volume:** 80% to 100%
- Adjust based on environment
- Lower for quiet places
- Higher for noisy environments

**Voice Selection:**
- **Female voices:** Better for children's stories, fairy tales
- **Male voices:** Better for adventure, action stories
- **Try different voices** to find your favorite!

## üåü Pro Tips

### For Best Narration Experience:

1. **Use headphones or earphones**
   - Better sound quality
   - More immersive
   - Blocks external noise

2. **Read along while listening**
   - Helps with comprehension
   - Good for learning
   - More engaging

3. **Adjust speed for your comfort**
   - Slower for learning new words
   - Faster if you're a confident reader
   - Normal for casual listening

4. **Choose right voice for story type**
   - Adventure: Male, faster speed
   - Fantasy: Female, normal speed
   - Educational: Clear voice, slower speed
   - Bedtime: Calm voice, slower speed

5. **Pre-load stories offline**
   - Download voice data first
   - Works without internet after
   - Smoother playback
